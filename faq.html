Who we are?

We are a manufacturer of computer games, designed for eye trackers with a view to children who have fount it difficult to play so far

Games

A set of games in our offer support communication in children with developmental challenges. The games are friendly and while they are used for therapy, they also provide lots of fun.

For who?

They are designed for children aged 6-13 years. For children with developmental problems or completly healthy.

Purpose

If only the child has challenges with eye contact or is also permanently immobilized then such apps can be a great therapy aid for the child.
